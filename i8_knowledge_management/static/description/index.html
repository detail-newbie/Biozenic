FAQ

Can I restrict editing/viewing per article?

Yes. Set access to everyone, a department, or a specific user. By default, view is open and edit is restricted.

Can I delete published articles?

No. Published content can be archived (admin) but not deleted to preserve the audit trail.

How are likes and views tracked?

Likes are stored per user; views are debounced to avoid inflating metrics.

Can I share an article publicly?

Yes — use the secure token-based link from the Share action in the toolbar menu.
